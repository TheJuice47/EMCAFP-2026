Eastern Mindanao Command

The Eastern Mindanao Command (EMCOM) is a unified command of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) responsible for the defense and security of the eastern part of Mindanao, which includes the regions of Caraga, Davao, and Soccsksargen. EMCOM's primary mission is to protect the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Philippines, as well as to maintain peace and order in its area of responsibility.
